### 2. Four Enhancement Protocols

**Protocol 1: Quantum Expertise Signaling**
- Adds "Neden-Sonuç" (Cause-Effect) causal proof chains
- Historical validation with accuracy percentages
- Example: "X verisi Y'yi tetikler, çünkü Z korelasyonu tarihsel olarak %90 doğrudur"
- E-E-A-T bonus: +3-5 points

**Protocol 2: Multi-Dimensional Trust System**
- Systematic source attribution for all data points
- Transparency layers with credibility scores (0-100)
- Format: "According to [Source] data from [Date], [Metric] shows [Value]"
- E-E-A-T bonus: +2-5 points

**Protocol 3: Semantic Entity Mapping**
- Expands from 3-5 to 20+ technical entities
- 5 categories: Market Indicators, On-Chain, Correlation, Technical, Macro
- Entity interconnection mapping with relationship types
- Multi-language definitions (6 languages)
- E-E-A-T bonus: +2-5 points

**Protocol 4: Predictive Sentiment Analysis**
- Predicts next emotional breakpoint with historical pattern matching
- Requires minimum 20 historical precedents
- Includes timeframe, trigger level, confidence score, risk factors
- Generates "SIA Sentiment Score" section
- E-E-A-T bonus: Included in Experience component

### 3. New Content Sections

**[AUTHORITY_MANIFESTO]** (50-75 words)
- Placed after headline, before Layer 1 (ÖZET)
- Establishes "Burası En Doğru Yer" authority within seconds
- Components: Authority establishment + Unique value proposition + Methodology transparency
- E-E-A-T bonus: +3-5 points

**[SEMANTIC_DEEP_DIVE]** (Enhanced Layer 2)
- Base SIA_INSIGHT enhanced with:
  - 20+ entity links with inline definitions
  - Causal reasoning chains with historical accuracy
  - Transparency layers with source attribution
  - Contrarian narrative from Phase 2
  - Sharp Language phrases

**[E-E-A-T_VERIFICATION]** (100-150 words)
- Placed after Layer 3 (DYNAMIC_RISK_SHIELD)
- 5 required components: Data Sources, Methodology, Confidence Level, Limitations, Disclaimer

- Ensures AdSense compliance

**[SIA_SENTIMENT_SCORE]** (Predictive Analysis)
- Placed at content end
- Current sentiment score + zone
- Next emotional breakpoint prediction
- Historical accuracy percentage
- Risk factors
